Benchmarks & Evaluation

Silent evaluates capabilities across decision quality, latency, and oversight alignment. Our benchmark suite focuses on:

  • Multimodal reasoning with provenance checks across 20+ public and private datasets.
  • Repository-scale code transformations validated through deterministic CI pipelines.
  • Governance scoring that simulates adversarial behaviors and containment triggers.

Each release includes reproducible runbooks so partners can validate metrics independently.

Methodology

Our methodology balances rapid iteration with provable oversight:

  1. Alignment-first design: Model objectives are codified as programmable guardrails before deployment.
  2. Human-in-the-loop orchestration: Silent stages tasks into checkpoints requiring human acknowledgement.
  3. Observable autonomy: Every action emits telemetry captured by Silent Aegis for review.

Partners receive access to methodology updates through our research briefings.

Known Limitations

We actively track and disclose Silent’s limitations to promote responsible use:

  • Silent defers to human oversight when encountering novel policy conflicts.
  • High-context tool integrations require onboarding sessions to ensure proper containment.
  • We rely on partner infrastructure for certain latency-sensitive integrations.

Submit feedback during early access to help us prioritize mitigations.
