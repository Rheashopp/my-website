FAQ

Can we adapt Aegis policies to regional regulations?

Absolutely. Start with Silent’s templates and layer your jurisdiction-specific rules.

How intrusive is the monitoring?

Aegis watches signals, not content, and only escalates when policy thresholds are crossed.

Does Aegis integrate with our incident tooling?

It connects to PagerDuty, ServiceNow, Slack, and SIEM platforms for seamless response orchestration.

Who benefits from Aegis dashboards?

Risk teams, compliance leaders, and executives receive role-based views tuned to their responsibilities.

What happens if the platform detects a high-risk event?

Aegis can trigger containment playbooks, require human acknowledgment, and preserve an immutable audit trail.
