What it does

  • Maps complex research questions into accountable plans with clear milestones.
  • Activates trusted tools to gather data, annotations, and citations automatically.
  • Delivers evidence-linked synthesis that keeps reviewers and regulators aligned.

How it works

  1. Captures your brief and decomposes it into policy-aware research threads.
  2. Coordinates data pulls and analysis via approved notebooks, APIs, and agents.
  3. Produces decision briefs with live provenance and exportable reference trails.

Features

FAQ

How does Orion keep results verifiable?

Each assertion is anchored to a signed evidence fragment with timestamps, so audits and peer review stay effortless.

Can Orion plug into our internal tools?

Yes. Orion ships with secure adapters and a policy sandbox for bespoke datasets and research workflows.

Who typically deploys Orion first?

Strategy, R&D, and intelligence teams that need fast synthesis without sacrificing traceability.

What oversight controls exist?

Human approvers gate key phases, and administrators can enforce policy packs per project.

Does Orion support multilingual corpora?

It supports multilingual ingestion and can translate sources while preserving original context.
